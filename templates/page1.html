WHAT IS DIFFERENTIAL PRIVACY?

01

Differential privacy mathematically guarantees that anyone seeing the result of a differentially private analysis will essentially make the same inference about any individual’s private information, whether or not that individual’s private information is included in the input to the analysis.

02

Ensuring privacy is crucial for numerous applications, including maintaining the integrity of sensitive information, eliminating the opportunity for adversaries to track users based on personally identifiable information (PII), etc. DP is appealing for a variety of applications because it guarantees the following privacy “needs”:

03

DP provides a mathematically provable guarantee of privacy protection against a wide range of privacy attacks (include differencing attack, linkage attacks, and reconstruction attacks).
